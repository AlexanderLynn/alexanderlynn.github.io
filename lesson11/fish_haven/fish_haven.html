Fish Haven News

Fish Haven! Located right off the coast of Bear Lake on the Utah Idaho borders, this is as far south as you get in Idaho. Tempuratures are warm here compared to the rest of the state. It makes up the second largest body of fresh water in the state of Utah.
We are known for our many state parks here. Wildlife, great fishing, Raspberry Festivals and more are abundant year round! Even when the weather isnt right for water skiing, bring your rifle and take up some hunting. This is the best weather we have in Idaho. Come enjoy!

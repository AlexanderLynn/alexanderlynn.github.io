Soda Springs News

Soda Springs located in Caribou County Idaho is a smaller town who natural wonders provide quite a sight to be had!
The town is know for the many springs of carbonated water dotting in and around the city (yes, in it as well). Native Americans were very familiar with this area long before it became a hot spot on the Oregon trail. Temperatures can be quite extreme here so make sure you pack a coat in the winter and shorts for the summer! The temperature differences year round can range over 130 F!

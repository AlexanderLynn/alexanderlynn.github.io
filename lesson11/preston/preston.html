Preston News

Ah preston. A more temperate area in comparison to a lot of Idaho's weather. This town is filled with history, some fascinating and others more sad.
Latter-day Saint Pioneers first entered into what is modern day Preston and Cache Valley in 1866. It was first named Worm Creek, but later has the name changed to Preston in honor of the Stake President William B Preston.
Come enjoy the welcome festivies including the festival of lights, "Napoleon Dynamite Festival" and many other great events. The town is growing quickly!

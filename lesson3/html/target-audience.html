Target Audience

Who: Those seeking minimalist the lifestyle
Age: 16-90
Income: All ranges
Motivation: Something very quick and easy to identify. A page they can count on to receive accurate weather updates and enjoy doing it.

Personas

John:
Occupation: Construction worker
Demographics and Education: 39 years old. Bachelor’s degree in construction Management.
Goals and Motivation: Works outside most of the day and goes on morning runs. He loves simple
living and doesn’t want to carry more than he needs to. A simple and quick way to view the weather is a must. If it looks good, even better.
Browser of choice: Mobile phone. Laptop, chrome.

Mary:
Occupation: Homemaker and flower arranger
Demographics and Education: 25 years old. Technical training in bouquet and flower arranging. Associates of Business.
Goals and Motivation: Mary is always taking care of her 2- and 3-year-old daughters in between her flower arrangements.
She runs the shop out of the spare bedroom in her home and is quite successful at it. She often takes her children with her
when picking up flowers for bouquets. The weather changes rapidly where she lives, and she needs a quick way to update herself for dressing her children.
Browser of choice: Mobile phone. Business iPad

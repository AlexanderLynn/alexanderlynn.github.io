Provo's History

Provo UT has a rich pioneer history. Home to Brigham Young University, formerly Brigham Young Academy, and one of the first major settlements in Utah, its history is robust and deep. Provo is growing quickly in many field, majorily information and internet technologies.
According to the Census, over 90% of people living in Provo has a high school education, and 43% have a bachelor's degree or higher. 65% of the population of Provo are working age with remarkably low un-employment. This city truly is a center of commerce and growth.

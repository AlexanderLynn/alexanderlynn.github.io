Want to join the Provo Chamber?

By joining the Provo Chamber of Commerce you are opening up your gates to recieving more employees and better talent! After being accepted a listing will appear on our website and the information will be added to our directory (for special advertising options please mention the word "special" in the business description).
Listing levels include the following features and build upon previous levels:

Non-Profit

  • Directory listings

Bronze

  • Everything in the Non-Profit level
  • Social media posts and virtual business trainings

Silver

  • Everything in the Bronze and Non-Profit levels
  • Access to our monthly in-person business trainings and tickets to our luncheons

Gold

  • Everything in the Silver, Bronze, and Non-Profit levels
  • Sponsorships and custom advertising options

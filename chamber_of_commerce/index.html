Why the Provo Chamber of Commerce?

The Provo Chamber of Commerce has existed for over half of a century and has connections to most major companies old a new. The employment opportunties with a connection here are fantastic.
Small to large business? We can help too. By becoming a corporate partner with the Chamber you are opening up another front for finding the best employees. Talent is often times harder to find; we can help connect. Our Goals are simple:

  • Help employers find the best talent
  • Help job seekers find the job opportunities they want
  • Serve the community
